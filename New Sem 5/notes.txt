Mujhe pata hai, hum dono mein bhi galtiyan hoti hain,
Par mujhe yakin hai, hum dono milke sab kuch theek karenge.
Meri khushiyan teri saath, aur mere aansu bhi teri saath,
Jitna tum samajh paate ho, utna koi aur nahi samajh sakta.

Meri zindagi mein ek nayi roshni tu hai,
Jab se tu mila, har raasta asaan ho gaya.
Teri muskurahat mein woh khaas baat hai,
Jo mere dil ki har udaasi ko door kar gayi.

Tu mera soulmate hai, mera har sapna tu,
Zindagi ki har khushi tujhse hi to hai.
Kuch palon mein nafrat, kuch mein pyaar ka ehsaas hai,
Par tu saath ho toh sab kuch beinteha behtareen lagta hai.

Jab shankaon ka saaya mere upar hota hai,
Meri aankhon se tujhko yaad karte hi,
Meri zindagi ke har dard ka shifa mil jaata hai,
Kyunki tu meri duniya ka sabse zaroori hissa hai.
